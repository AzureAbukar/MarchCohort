Cooking

This will take approximately 35 mins to prep.
Additionally, it will take around 30 mins to cook

    1. Step 1:
    • pre heat the oven to 160 on fan
    • Oil and line base and sides of cake tins with baking parchment
    • Into a jugg add and whisk:
      • 230 ml of Oil
      • 100g natural yoghurt
      • 4 large eggs
      • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
      • zested orange
    2. Step 2:
    • Into a bowl add and mix:
      • 265g of flour
      • 335g of sugar
      • 2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
      • 1/4 nutmeg
    3. Step 3:
    • Squeeze any lumps of sugar through your fingers
    • shake the bowl a few times to bring the lumps to the surface
    4. Step 4:
    • Add wet ingredients to the dry, along with carrots, raisins , nuts
    • Mix well to combine, then divide between the lines
    5. Step 5:
    • Bake for half an hour or until a skewer insterted into the centre comes out clean
